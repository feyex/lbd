WHO WE ARE


LBD International Limited was incorporated in 1992 as a limited liability company. We are the First Nigerian indigenous Company to have such trusted industry relations with the Oil majors. With over 25 years’ experience in the Nigerian Business environment, LBD International Limited is capable of brokering Oil & Gas deals both locally and Internationally.

We have supplied OCTG and line pipe worth tens of millions of Dollars to the Multinational and Indigenous Oil Companies in Nigeria for Onshore and Deepwater Operations.Our vision is to consistently exceed our clients’ expectation by delivering the best quality tubular services in a most professional and ethical manner.

We have established a successful track record of managing and delivering top quality tubular solutions through a proven and effective business model.Our clients include most of Nigeria’s leading Oil & Gas companies engaged in offshore and onshore services. Our principal products include OCTG (casing, tubing) and line pipes.We are committed to our clients’ satisfaction.

WHY LBD

LBD is a one stop shop.We Deliver FOB worldwide, CIF Nigerian port or DDP (including customs and clearing) to your required delivery point in Nigeria.

We have a supply chain network of both international and local suppliers. We are suppliers of a full range of tubular products, both OCTG and line pipe, either direct from the mill for long delivery requirements or from our stocks in Europe, Dubai, USA & China for urgent delivery requirements.

Our business operations are strictly performed with the objective of ensuring customer satisfaction We are in compliance with Nigerian laws and regulations. (DPR, NIPEX, NCDMB) Our company is renowned for delivering products that meet industry standards. We always put our clients first.
